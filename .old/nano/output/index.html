word, sound & prosody

Joshua Mostafa writes fiction, essays, poetry and lyrics.

I'm interested in the spaces between things:
prose and poetry, idea and narrative, the written and the spoken word.

I'm a doctoral student at the Writing & Society Research Centre, researching the representation of prehistory in creative writing. My friend Seb and I run the record label Inna Riddim, and I host the Altitude 507 music show on Orbital Radio.

This is my personal website. Elsewhere on the web, you can find my academic papers, bookshelves, references, record crates, DJ mixes and recipes.

Feel free to contact me.
